- **OpenDyslexic font** - Research shows it doesn't work

**Problem:** No free comprehensive dyslexia intervention programs exist

## Who This Serves

### Primary Target: Low-Income Families
- **4-5 million** low-income US students have dyslexia
- Cannot afford $100-600/month for intervention
- Research quote: *"For many parents, the enormous cost typically associated with specialized instruction is one of the most troubling aspects of having their child diagnosed with dyslexia"*

### Secondary Targets
- Schools with limited budgets
- Rural/remote families (limited access to specialists)
- International users (many programs US-only or expensive)
- Families waiting for school services (often 6-12 month wait)
- Homeschooling families
